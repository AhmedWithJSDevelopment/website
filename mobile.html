What is custom mobile app development?
Custom app development is the process of designing, developing, and releasing a unique mobile app based on the specific business, technical and design requirements. Unlike off-the-shelf solutions, custom applications are created to meet specific customer needs in a particular way.
How long does it take to develop a mobile app?
It depends on the requirements and resources available. According to BusinessOfApps, on the average mobile app development takes:
1.1-3 months for MVP with a simple design and a back-end and basic functionality;
2.4-8 months for mandatory functions, advanced UX/UI, API integrations, and server performance;
3.9+ months for special features and additional functionality.
Each phase requires a different amount of time to complete, but rough estimates are:
  • Gathering project requirements: 1-2 weeks
  • Analyzing needs and selecting technologies: 4-5 weeks
  • Design and prototyping: 6-12 weeks
  • Coding: 6-12 weeks
  • Releasing to app stores: 1-2 weeks
Any improvements to the mobile app take additional time, so keep in mind that the development process doesn’t end immediately after the release.
What technologies should I choose for mobile app development?
The tech stack for your mobile app project is highly dependent on your demands, the platform, and the type of mobile development. If you want to make a mobile app that runs smoothly on Android and iOS, we recommend choosing React Native or Flutter as your core technologies.
