Some of our successful cases

FAG

Do you do adaptive design?
Yes, our UI/UX design expertise covers adaptive and responsive design. Whether you need to retrofit your website to make it more mobile-friendly or looking to build a brand new one, we will equip it with great performance and exceptional user experience on any browser or device.
What data do you need from me before working on my project?
A good start for a UI/UX design project is to clarify your business goals, the styles you prefer (samples or references), the platforms you will use (desktop/mobile), and whether you're looking for a custom or ready-made solution.
With a clear understanding of your vision, well-defined requirements, and a target audience to work with, we will get back with a plan, estimates, and first drafts in a few days from the point we have received your request.
What type of businesses do you work with?
Our UI/UX design company provides a full range of UI/UX design services for businesses of all types and sizes. Our clients' list includes fast-growing startups and established Fortune 500 сompanies.
Which tools do your UX/UI company use for UI/UX design?
Here are the tools our employees use for UI/UX design:
  • Sketch
  • Adobe XD
  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Figma and more
What is included in mobile design services?
Our mobile app design services include user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) designing. We create the overall style of the app, including the logo, colors, fonts, and other graphic elements.
First, we make wireframes, then develop 2-3 visual concepts, design the app screen by screen, and create a clickable prototype.
What do web design services involve?
First, we study your goals and gather requirements, analyze user behavior, and look through existing solutions on the market.
Next, we create wireframes – layouts that show where elements are placed – in Sketch and InVision.
Finally, we develop two or three visual concepts you can choose from, design the website screen by screen, and present its clickable prototype.
How many design revisions do clients get?
Usually, we fully satisfy the requirements of our clients for projects after one to three rounds of improvements. However, the number of modifications you receive depends on your chosen project's collaboration model.
What other services do you provide except design?
Relevant is a full-cycle software development company with extensive business and technical experience. We can do everything from planning and design to developing, testing, and maintaining your web, mobile, or desktop products and services.
Our UI/UX design company offers flexible collaboration models, such as dedicated teams and staff augmentation, so you can always complement your project with designers, project managers, business analysts, and developers.
When you hire at least one designer from Relevant, you gain access to all of our employees' professional input and expertise. Beyond design, we can provide you with feedback and valuable information about your project's business and technical aspects to help you succeed.
What steps must I follow to hire a UI/UX specialist?
The steps to hire a UI/UX designer from Relevant are very simple. Just send your project requirements. We share the most appropriate candidate's profile with you for an interview based on your project requirements. The shortlisted UI/UX specialist is deployed to work as an offshore team member.
