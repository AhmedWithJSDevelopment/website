Why do companies need DevOps?
DevOps enables organizations to efficiently implement new features and maintain stability to support digital transformation. However, these are not all the benefits. DevOps provides a culture shift that removes the barriers between development and operations and allows teams to assess the scope of the entire system and evaluate the impact of needed changes. As a result, the company reacts faster to changes, and this reaction benefits end-users.
What are the key principles of DevOps?
DevOps, in a nutshell, is the uniting of people, processes, and tools aimed to provide value to its customers. The key principles of any DevOps solutions and service provider include enhanced collaboration, continuous improvement, automating routine processes, and customer-centric culture.
What is the main goal of DevOps?
The primary goal of DevOps is to speed up the time to market, ensure a more streamlined development process, and improve team collaboration across the value stream. Often, companies require an experienced DevOps solutions provider to achieve the described benefits.
Do you provide cloud-based software development and deployment?
Cloud-based DevOps tools help make software development predictable and reliable. Our DevOps team offers cloud infrastructure deployment, including private, public, and hybrid clouds. We also provide IaaS services and pay-as-you-go access to storage, networking, servers, and other computing resources in the cloud (Amazon AWS, Google, Microsoft Azure).
What affects the project costs?
The DevOps services cost may vary from project to project; however, you can see a standard list of things affecting prices below.
  • Project scope and complexity
  • The chosen technology
  • Project completion urgency
  • Engagement model: Time and Material or Dedicated Team
  • The number of end-users
